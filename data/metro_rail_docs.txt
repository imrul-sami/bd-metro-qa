# Metro Rail Overview
Bangladesh Metro Rail is a modern mass transit system in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It aims to reduce traffic congestion and improve transportation efficiency.

# Ticket Purchase
Passengers can buy Metro Rail tickets from ticket counters, vending machines, or through an online app. Smart cards are also available for regular users.

# Operating Hours
Metro Rail operates from 7 AM to 10 PM daily. Trains arrive every 5-10 minutes depending on the time of day.

# Metro Stations
Key stations include Uttara, Pallabi, Mirpur, Farmgate, Karwan Bazar, Shahbagh, and Motijheel.

# Safety Features
The Metro Rail system is equipped with CCTV cameras, emergency alarms, and dedicated security personnel for passenger safety.

# Future Expansion
The Metro Rail network plans to expand to other areas of Dhaka and connect to major suburbs.
